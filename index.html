Why is it important for science to be effective?

Primary outcome of quality scientific research is a product with high added value with a potential to affect our daily lives. Internet may be a good example how scientific discovery can change world as was known before.

Countries with high standard of living and healthcare have something in common - quality scientific research. As recent Covid-19 pandeminc shown, countries, where science is in the centre of interest, were able to better handle such complex challenges when comapred to countries which do not consider science as their priority.

When facing climate changes, science should provide answers rather than politics. Science produces reproducible verificable data, while politics depends on individual ethical and moral principles which is difficult to measure and standardize accross different cultures.

Our mission

The main goal of this project to devise a framework and/or tools which would make scientific processes more efficient and participation in them more available. Sciber tries to achieve this by finding a solutions which would satisfy following criteria:

Simplicity

Simple solution is easier to create and therefore it represents better starting goal for a development. Simple solution driven by simple rules can still form complex systems and produce sophisticated results. Also when available, simple solution is expected be more accessible for the wider community.

Universality

Improving common part of scientific process or providing a scientific tool which is universally applicable would potentially have more users and should have impact on bigger number branches of science.

Openness

Solution should make the scientific process inclusive for everyone who wants to participate. Making scientific processes open for participation then leads to their higher efficiency and effectiveness.

Adaptability

Provided tool should be not only versatile but also flexible to adapt to unknown needs in the future. The adaptability should be the tool's intrinsic property, so that it would be from start expected to be changed according any future requirements.

To fulfill above mentioned criteria, final solution should be made by combination of internet technologies with machine learning.
